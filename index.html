FAQ

Does this make me GDPR/CCPA compliant?

No. It’s a helpful start. Full compliance depends on your data flows, legal bases, and how you implement user rights & security.

Can I use this for my mobile app?

Yes. You can adapt the wording for apps, web services, or desktop software.

Do I need a cookie banner?

If you use non‑essential cookies (e.g., analytics/ads) in many jurisdictions you should ask consent before setting them.
